Featured Rafting Trips

Rapid waters

Embark on an epic journey through the majestic Rapid waters! This multi-day expedition takes you deep into the heart of one of the world's most awe-inspiring natural wonders. Experience thrilling whitewater rapids, explore hidden side canyons, hike to breathtaking viewpoints, and camp under a blanket of stars. Our experienced guides ensure your safety and provide fascinating insights into the canyon's geology, history, and ecology. All meals and camping equipment are provided. Learn More

Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

Difficulty: Challenging (Class IV)

Best Time to Go: April - October

Clear Creek Whitewater Rush

Get your adrenaline pumping on the fast-paced waters of Clear Creek! Located just a short drive from Orfino (Note: Adjust location if your company is elsewhere), this half-day trip is perfect for thrill-seekers looking for an exciting whitewater experience. Navigate exhilarating Class III rapids with our expert guides. No prior rafting experience is necessary, but a sense of adventure is recommended! All necessary safety gear is provided. Learn More

Duration: Half Day (4 hours)

Difficulty: Moderate (Class III)

Best Time to Go: June - August

Nebraska Smooth Waters

Enjoy a relaxing and scenic float down a calmer section of the beautiful Nebraska River. This full-day trip is ideal for families and those looking for a more leisurely experience. Soak in the stunning desert landscapes, spot local wildlife, and enjoy a delicious riverside lunch. Our knowledgeable guides will share interesting facts about the area's natural and cultural history. This trip requires minimal paddling effort. Learn More

Duration: Full Day (6-8 hours)

Difficulty: Easy (Class I-II)

Best Time to Go: May - September
